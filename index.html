About Us

REMedy is a mobile application that simplifies sleep data into clear, actionable insights. By connecting with health trackers like Apple Health, Oura Ring, and Fitbit, as well as user calendar data, the app helps users understand their sleep patterns and develop better habits. It functions as both a temporary sleep coach (typically over an 8-week period) and an educational resource, providing users with relevant information when they need it most.

The app uses SMART goal-setting principles and focuses on sustainable habit formation throughout the day, not just at bedtime. It sends timely reminders about factors affecting sleep, like caffeine intake and screen time, based on each user's schedule. Rather than overwhelming users with data, REMedy filters information to highlight the most relevant insights for improving sleep quality. The app offers both free and premium features, with the basic version focusing on habit tracking and foundational sleep education, while premium features include access to sleep experts and product recommendations.
