About Us

At REMedy, our mission is to help people get serious about their sleep. Today, health-tech and wearable device advancements have enabled us to collect vast amounts of high-quality sleep data. However, through 100+ customer interviews, we've learned that this data often does not lead to the meaningful behavior or habit changes that people expect.

This is where sleep coaches and consultants come in! Demand for sleep guidance is growing, but many individuals struggle to find a coach who suits their needs. We aim to connect people who already track their sleep health data, whether for occupational necessity or personal reasons, with experienced coaches who can provide science-backed insights, accountability, and encouragement to help convert data into real, sustainable change.
